CAMEL :

INTERESTING FACTS ABOUT CAMEL

  • There are two types of camels: One humped or “dromedary” camels and two humped Bactrian camels.
  • Their humps let them store up to 80 pounds of fat which they can live off for weeks and even months.
  • When a camel finally does find water, he can drink up to 40 gallons in one go.

    • CHAMELEON :

    INTERESTING FACTS ABOUT CHAMELEON

  • Chameleons are a very unique branch of the lizard group of reptiles.
  • Special color pigment cells under the skin called chromatophores allow some chameleon species to change their skin color blue, yellow and purple,etc.
  • There are around 160 species of chameleon on this planet.

    • PIGEON :

    INTERESTING FACTS ABOUT PIGEON

  • Pigeons can easily detect distant storms, earthquakes and volcanic eruptions since they have a very sharp sense of hearing.
  • Lifespan of average Pigeon is around 15 years.
  • Pigeons are the highly intelligent animal and can fly at the greater speed because of their strong wings as fast as 70 to 80 miles per hour.

    • PANDA :

    INTERESTING FACTS ABOUT PANDA

  • They can reach 6 ft (1.9 m) in height and in the wild weigh around 220–250 lb (110–115 kg), although adult males can weigh up to 350 lbs (160 kg).
  • Pandas go from pink to white and black or brown.
  • An adult can eat 12–38 kilos of bamboo per day!
